Spiced Orange Juice

Back to Home

Description

This recipe is inspired by the cold nights of Alaskan winters. It is tangy and has a zest of orange to keep you warm and out going.

Ingredients

  • TANG orange juice
  • 250 ml of warm water
  • a cinnamon stick and a mulled wine condiment mix(optional)

Steps

  1. Get a table spoon of TANG orange juice
  2. Add the mix in the cup
  3. Pour the warm water over and give it a good stir
  4. Enjoy your drink out in the cold or at the safety of a warm fireplace!
