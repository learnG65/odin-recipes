Sweet noodles with milk

Back to Home

Description

Sweet and cozy, this is what we are proposing for our next recipe. Get ready to get sleepy and doze off after a hard day with our sweet noodles

Ingredients

  • 500g noodles or vermicelli (preferably whole wheat for added nutrients)
  • 1 liter of milk (you can use whole milk for a richer taste or plant-based milk for a vegan option)
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar (this adds a caramelized note)
  • 1 packet vanilla sugar (for an enticing aroma)
  • 1-2 tablespoons cinnamon (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, but recommended)
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract (for a sophisticated flavor)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract (to balance the sweetness)
  • Zest of one orange (for an extra freshness)

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Make sure all ingredients are at hand. The noodles can be broken into smaller pieces for better integration into the mixture.
  2. Boil the milk: Take a large pot and place the milk over medium heat. Add the brown sugar and vanilla sugar, stirring gently to dissolve.
  3. Add the flavors: When the milk starts to boil, add the vanilla, almond, and lemon extracts, as well as the cinnamon. The smell will be simply divine!
  4. Incorporate the noodles: Add the broken noodles to the hot milk. Stir well and let simmer on low heat for 10-12 minutes, or until the noodles are fully cooked and have absorbed most of the milk.
  5. Add the orange zest: In the last minutes of cooking, add the orange zest to intensify the flavor of the dessert.
  6. Serving: Once the dessert is ready, you can choose to serve it warm or cold, depending on your preferences. It can be garnished with a sprinkle of cinnamon or chopped nuts for added texture.
